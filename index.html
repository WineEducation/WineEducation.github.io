In-Home Tastings

When I lived in France, a friend of mine would teach me about wine while we sat around the table, cooked meals, and hung out on the patio. The experience marked me deeply, and it's one I hope to share with you. We should all get to learn about wine the way I did, in our own space with those we want there with us.

This is a bespoke experience, completely customizable to your tastes. There really is nothing else like it. We will discuss your vision for the event ahead of time, so that you, your friends, and your guests will have a remarkable experience.

The price is $75 per person, not including wine.

Events & Trainings

If you own or manage a wine tasting room, I provide learning sessions for your patrons using your wines to help them gain valuable wine knowledge. This allows you to provide a unique benefit to your guests that will set you apart. Your terroir and your wines will be the plumb line that marks their basis for wine knowledge.

I also provide trainings for tasting room associates. They will learn the basics of what influences the character of wine. Your associates will be able to provide a robust conversation that will set your service apart.
